WHAT IS THIS?
DSA Leveling is a Solo Leveling–inspired tracker for your DSA practice. Log every problem you attempt, earn XP, rank up from E to Shadow Monarch, and stay sharp with confidence-based spaced repetition review.
HOW TO EARN XP
Solve Easy+20 XP
Solve Medium+50 XP
Solve Hard+100 XP
Bonus Easy (beyond daily target)+10 XP
Bonus Medium (beyond daily target)+25 XP
Bonus Hard (beyond daily target)+50 XP
Focus — Scout Sprint (25m)+50 XP
Focus — Hunter Grind (45m)+65 XP
Focus — Dungeon Push (60m)+80 XP
Focus — Boss Raid (90m)+120 XP
Daily Quest: Solve (×daily target)+20×n XP
Daily Quest: Log (×daily target)+10×n XP
Daily Quest: Focus session+50 XP
Review: Easy rating+5 XP

CONFIDENCE & SPACED REVIEW
Rate your confidence 1–5 when logging a problem. This controls how soon it comes back for review — lower confidence means more frequent repetition.

1 → review in 1 day  ·  2 → 2 days  ·  3 → 5 days  ·  4 → 10 days  ·  5 → 21 days

When reviewing, rating yourself adjusts your confidence ±1 automatically and reschedules the next review date.
FEATURE GUIDE
DashboardOverview of rank, streak, solved count, total XP, daily quests, and 365-day activity heatmap.
Problem LogLog every problem with name, topic, difficulty, status, time, platform, language, confidence, approach, code, and notes. Password-protected edits and deletes.
Skill TreeVisualizes mastery per topic based on how many problems you've solved in it. Statuses: Locked → Novice → Adept → Master.
Spaced ReviewShows problems due for review today, sorted by lowest confidence first. Rate each attempt to reschedule.
Focus ModeFour named timer presets: Scout Sprint (25m), Hunter Grind (45m), Dungeon Push (60m), Boss Raid (90m). Each awards XP on completion.
Daily QuestsThree quests per day: solve N problems, log N problems, complete 1 focus session — where N matches your registered daily target. Claim XP when done.
BackupExport your progress as a JSON file. Import it on any device to restore your data. Sheet problems are excluded from backup (they regenerate automatically).
TIPS
· Log even failed attempts — they count toward quests and track your journey
· Use topic autocomplete for consistent skill tree tracking
· Bonus XP kicks in only after you've hit your daily target for the day
· Lower confidence problems appear first in review — tackle your weak spots
· Export your backup regularly; it only saves your problem logs and progress, not sheet data
· Your password protects all edit and delete operations — don't forget it
